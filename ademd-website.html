The Challenge

You Didn't Train for a Field That Changes This Fast

Metabolic medicine is transforming at a pace that no textbook, no single journal, and no solo practice can keep up with.

You're seeing patients with complex, overlapping conditions every day — and the associations that should help? Too often they deliver dense theory, expensive travel, and a badge for your wall.

  • "I can't keep up with how fast this field is moving." — GLP-1s, CGM tech, shifting obesity guidelines: all at once.
  • "I know what the guidelines say, but applying them to complex patients is a different story."
  • "I feel like I'm practicing in a silo — there's no one to talk through hard cases with."
  • "I never got enough training in obesity medicine. It just wasn't covered."

You don't need more CME checkboxes. You need to walk into your next appointment more confident than you walked into the last one.

About ADEMD

The Association Built for the Way Metabolic Medicine Actually Works

The Association for Diabetes, Endocrine, and Metabolic Disorders (ADEMD) exists for one reason: to make you a sharper, more connected clinician — faster.

We're the only professional home that brings together diabetes specialists, endocrinologists, obesity medicine physicians, researchers, and public health practitioners under one roof — because your patients don't fit neatly into one specialty either.

Led by world-class faculty and backed by partner institutions at the forefront of metabolic science, ADEMD is where the field's sharpest minds choose to learn — and to teach.

FAQ

Questions Clinicians Ask Before Joining

Everything you need to know before you register — answered plainly, without the jargon.

Is ADEMD only for endocrinologists?
Not at all. ADEMD is built for the full metabolic care team — endocrinologists, diabetes specialists, obesity physicians, scientists, nurse practitioners, physician assistants, and public health practitioners. If your work touches diabetes, endocrine health, or metabolic disorders, you belong here.
What makes ADEMD events different from standard CME?
Most CME gives you credits. ADEMD gives you that plus a peer network, world-class faculty, and sessions designed around real clinical decisions — not just academic updates. You'll leave each event with specific, practical steps you can apply immediately in your practice.
How do I know the content will be relevant to my practice?
Our programming is shaped by practicing clinicians, not committees. Every topic on our agenda reflects the questions you and your peers are actually asking — from the latest GLP-1 evidence to managing complex comorbid cases. If it's moving the field, it's on our stage.
Can I attend events if I'm outside the US?
Absolutely. ADEMD has global reach. Most of our webinars and masterclasses are offered virtually, so you can participate regardless of where you practice. Our in-person summits are held in major medical hubs — and recordings are available to all members.
